About the Open House

Explore the details, objectives, and what participants will experience at the BU IT Department Open House 2024.

Details about the Event

The BU IT Department Open House 2024 will be held from August 29-31, 2024, at Bangkok University. This event offers a unique opportunity for students, tech enthusiasts, and professionals to explore the latest developments in the field of IT, meet industry leaders, and engage in hands-on workshops. Participants will have the chance to learn about a variety of cutting-edge topics such as full-stack development, data science, AI, machine learning, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

Objectives of the Event

The primary objectives of the Open House are to:

  • Introduce attendees to emerging technologies and innovations in the field of IT.
  • Provide hands-on workshops to enhance technical skills in web development, AI, data science, and more.
  • Create networking opportunities with experts, instructors, and industry professionals.
  • Showcase the capabilities and achievements of Bangkok University's IT Department.

What Participants Will Experience

During the event, participants can expect:

  • Interactive workshops led by industry professionals and Bangkok University professors.
  • Insightful talks on the latest trends in IT, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.
  • A networking session where attendees can engage with peers and experts.
  • Exhibits and demonstrations showcasing real-world applications of AI, machine learning, and web development.
